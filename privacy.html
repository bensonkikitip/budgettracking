Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Slo N Ready ("the app") is a personal budget tracker for iOS. Your privacy is the foundation of how this app is built.

We collect no data

The app does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs that track you.

Everything stays on your device

All data you enter — transactions, categories, budgets, and rules — is stored locally in a SQLite database on your iPhone or iPad. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself using the built-in backup feature.

No network requests

The app makes zero network requests. It does not connect to the internet at any point during normal use.

Backups

The app automatically saves a backup file to your device's local storage. You can export this file to your own iCloud Drive, AirDrop it to another device, or share it however you choose — the app has no involvement beyond writing the file locally.

No third parties

No data is shared with any third party. There are no advertising networks, crash reporters, or analytics services integrated into the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on GitHub.
